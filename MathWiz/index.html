Directions:

Cast a spell to damage the monster! The spells recipe is located in the right hand corner. Click the plus or minus buttons in your spell book to add the ingredients to match the spell. For more gems multiply the spell by the amount of damage you want to induce. Hurry, there is a timer that you must cast the spell before it runs out! The gems you earn during this game you can use to buy your avatar items in the avatar store!! Good luck and go get those monsters!
